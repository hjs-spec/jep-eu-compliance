The EU AI Act has staged application dates and may be further interpreted by implementing acts, delegated acts, harmonized standards, Commission guidance, national competent authorities, market surveillance authorities, notified bodies, courts, and other official sources.

This repository does not maintain an authoritative legal timeline.

Always consult:

- national competent authority guidance;
- qualified legal counsel.
